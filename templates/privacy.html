Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or link to your bank accounts.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your records. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all your data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces — this causes your browser to make a request to Google's servers when you visit the site. We do not integrate with any other third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please get in touch using the contact information available on the site. We will do our best to respond promptly.
